Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Photo Organizer. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Contact our support team.

Photo Organizer operates 100% offline. All file processing happens locally on your device, and your photos never leave your computer. We don't collect any personal data, usage analytics, or require internet connectivity. Your privacy is completely protected.

Photo Organizer supports all major photo formats including JPEG, PNG, HEIC, TIFF, and RAW files from all major camera manufacturers. For videos, it supports MP4, MOV, AVI, and other common formats. The app reads EXIF metadata to intelligently organize your files.

Photo Organizer offers both copy and move modes. We recommend using copy mode first to test the organization structure before committing to moving files. There's also a preview section that allows you to see the results before processing.

The app uses advanced hash algorithms to identify duplicate files by analyzing their content, not just filenames. It can find duplicates even if they have different names, are in different folders, or have slightly different metadata. You can choose to keep, ignore, or rename duplicates based on your preferences.

There's no artificial limit imposed by the software. The actual limit depends on your system's available memory and storage space. For optimal performance with large collections, we recommend processing folders with fewer than 5,000 files at once.

Mac: macOS 10.15 (Catalina) or later, 4GB RAM minimum (8GB recommended for large collections).
Windows: Windows 10 or Windows 11, 4GB RAM minimum (8GB recommended for large collections).
